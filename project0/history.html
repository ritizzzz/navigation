History

Noam Chomsky is an American linguist born in 1928. It all started when Noam Chomsky published his work on syntactic structure in 1957. In Syntactic structure, Chomsky proposed a new way to look at the theory of grammar and Chomsky worked out the theory of the universal grammar. From there, he went to state that the universal grammar is already pre-programmed in the human brain hence the innate approach was born.
